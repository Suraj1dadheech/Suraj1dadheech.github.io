Demand and Supply

According to WHO, population need is the number blood units required to transfuse all who require transfusion in a defined population over a defined time period.

Covid-19 News

The COVID-19 pandemic compromises blood donations endangering the life-saving blood supply. These alarming results offer evidence-based grounds for practical implications for driving donations in the event of a pandemic.
